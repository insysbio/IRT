How to

Brief description how to work with IRT:

  1. Open the section "Scheme" in the upper menu.
  2. Explore the schemes with the immune cells, cytokines, surface molecules, processes, interactions that are included in the IRT.
  3. Choose the species and processes that are necessary for your model by adding it to the stack.
  4. Download the model template of your choice in sbml format.
