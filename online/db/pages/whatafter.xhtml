Recommendations for templates use

Recommendations how to use model templates downloaded from IRT are presented below:

  1. Open the downloaded model template of your choice in modeling software that you use (for example, SimBiology).
  2. Add variables (cells, cytokines etc), reactions (equations) that are necessary for your model and that were absent in IRT.
  3. Identify unknown parameters related to the added equations and unknown parameters related to reactions taken from IRT.
  4. Apply the model.
